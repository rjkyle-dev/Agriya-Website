About Us

Agriya, located in Panabo City, is a visionary community that blends modern living with sustainable farming. Inspired by the city’s rich agricultural heritage as the "Banana Capital of the Philippines," Agriya offers a balanced lifestyle where urban convenience meets rural serenity. With features such as agri-tourism attractions, modern residences, an educational hub, and thoughtfully planned commercial spaces, Agriya fosters a thriving environment for families, businesses, and communities. It is home to the University of Southeastern Philippines – Agriya Campus and promotes sustainability through eco-friendly practices like rainwater harvesting and green spaces. At Agriya, innovation and agriculture come together to create a vibrant community that connects people to nature and the future.
